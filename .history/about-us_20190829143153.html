Our Mission

Our mission is to provide school equipment to developing countires in need. With IRSR, you can track and broadcast the needs of your school to board members in the US.

IRSR gives you the ability to login, create, read, update and delete information on the condition of the school including general issues and the condition of equipment.

DRC Rural Schools

Since our organization was founded we've tried different ways to organize ourselves with mixed results. With this software everything is easily accesible to our employees. Our management costs have lowered 15% allowing us to allocate more funds back into our classrooms.
